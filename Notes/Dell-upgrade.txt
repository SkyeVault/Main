Upgrade Parts List:

- GPU: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4070 12GB GDDR6X

- CPU Upgrade (optional): Intel Xeon E5-1650 v2 (6 cores, 3.5 GHz base, 3.9 GHz turbo)
