Quick Tips for Setting Up Three.js

  • Use module-based imports carefully: Always use full URLs or relative paths like ./three.module.js
  • Add type="module" to your script tag:
<script type="module" src="./main.js"></script>
  • Size your canvas properly:
renderer.setSize(window.innerWidth, window.innerHeight);
  • Set your camera position:
camera.position.z = 5;
  • Connect OrbitControls to your renderer:
const controls = new OrbitControls(camera, renderer.domElement);
