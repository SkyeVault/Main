Make a Fabric Book Full of Animal Stories

Rainkeep Animal Friends is a cut and sew fabric book designed for imaginative play and hands-on learning. Each habitat includes animals your child can move, match, and use for storytelling.

Includes: six habitat scenes and 18 animal characters for interactive play. Order one yard, cut out the pieces, sew the pages, and build a keepsake book.

Cut, sew, and play. No pattern drafting required.

Quick Start Guide

  1. Order 1 yard of Rainkeep Animal Friends fabric.
  2. Cut out the pages and animal pieces along the printed lines.
  3. Sew pages (add batting or interfacing if you want extra structure).
  4. Finish edges and enjoy storytime play.
