Syrenwork Online Gallery is Live

> Log Date: 2025-04-09

I just added a brand new gallery page to arynwood.com/syrenwork that showcases my artwork and connects all my creative outlets in one place.

The Syrenwork gallery page is officially live! This new section of my site is dedicated to showcasing my visual art across multiple platforms. I used Pictorem’s built-in iframe tool to embed a live preview of my online gallery, and added direct links to my:

  • Pictorem Print Shop
  • Etsy Store
  • Instagram Gallery
  • Spoonflower Patterns

It feels good to see everything in one place—a digital hub for Syrenwork that makes it easier to share my art with the world.

Next Steps

I may style this page more in the future to better match my cyberpunk aesthetic, or embed additional galleries using Spoonflower collections or a custom lightbox. But for now, the focus is function: it's clean, direct, and gets people where they want to go.
