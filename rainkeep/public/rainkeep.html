Quick Start Guide

  1. Order 1 yard on 56" Organic Cotton Sateen
  2. Cut out each character and piece along the lines
  3. Place pieces together, sew around edges (leave small opening)
  4. Stuff your toys with fiberfill
  5. Close with a ladder stitch and enjoy!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a cut and sew fabric book?

A cut and sew fabric book is a printed yard of fabric featuring complete toy patterns with seam allowances already included. You simply cut out the pieces, sew them together, stuff them, and create finished soft toys or plushies without needing separate patterns or templates.

How much fabric do I need to order?

You only need 1 yard of fabric to create the complete Rainkeep Village set. We recommend ordering it printed on 56" wide Organic Cotton Sateen for the best quality and ease of sewing.

Is this suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! Rainkeep Village is designed for sewers of all skill levels. The patterns include clear cutting lines and seam allowances, and basic straight stitching is all you need. If you can sew a straight line, you can make these adorable stuffed toys!

What supplies do I need besides fabric?

You'll need basic sewing supplies: scissors or rotary cutter, sewing machine or needle and thread, pins, and fiberfill stuffing. Everything else is printed right on your fabric!

Can I order custom characters that look like my family?

Yes! I offer custom family character sets. Simply email me at loreleihnoble@gmail.com with details about your family, and I'll create personalized cut and sew patterns featuring characters that resemble your loved ones.

How long does it take to make the toys?

Most people complete their first character in 1-2 hours once the fabric arrives. With practice, you can make each toy in about 30-45 minutes. It's a wonderful weekend project or rainy day activity with kids!

Are there instructions included?

Yes! Basic sewing instructions are provided, and we also offer free companion storybook pages that provide additional context and storytelling ideas for your finished characters.
