Print + Sew Instructions

  1. Order 1 yard printed on 56" Organic Cotton Sateen.
  2. When it arrives, cut out the pattern for each character and piece.
  3. Place matching pieces together, sew around edges (leave a small opening), then trim corners.
  4. Turn right-side out and gently stuff the characters.
  5. Close openings with a ladder stitch (or tight straight stitch) and enjoy your set.
