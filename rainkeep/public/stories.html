Stories to Inspire the Imagination

A companion page for Rainkeep Miniatures. Use these prompts as gentle starting points for cozy, imaginative storytelling with your child. Mix, match, and change any detail to make the stories your own.

Meet the Rainkeep Friends

These ideas are meant to work with the dolls and scenes in your Rainkeep fabric book. Feel free to rename anyone to match your family and your child’s imagination.

Artists & Makers
• Selene – the stained glass artist at Pebble & Prism Glass Studio
• Bram – the gentle woodworker who carves toys and furniture

Grandparents & Their Shops
• Nana Mira – the seamstress who runs the fabric & crochet studio
• Pop Jasper – the florist who fills the village with flowers
• Granny Elen – the baker who makes sweets at the deli & café
• Grandpa Corwin – the alchemist who mixes potions and glowing tonics

Magical Friends
• Wren – the village wizard
• Starbloom – the shimmering unicorn
• Lark & Pippin – two curious little fairies

Children & Companion
• Ivy – a brave little girl who loves adventures
• Mara – her imaginative friend who loves to help and plan
• Theo – a thoughtful friend who asks big questions
• Pip – the loyal dachshund who follows them everywhere

25 Story Seeds to Explore Together

  1. 1. The First Night in the Hobbit House

    Ivy and Mara arrive in Rainkeep for the very first time. Together you can decide what they pack, which bed they choose, and how they feel as they fall asleep under the stained glass windows in their new bedroom.

  2. 2. Pip the Dachshund’s Grand Tour

    Pip decides to explore every room in the house. Move Pip from the bedroom, to the kitchen, to the library and music room. What does Pip sniff, discover, and accidentally knock over along the way?

  3. 3. Morning at Pebble & Prism Glass Studio

    Selene is working on a new stained glass window for the village library. Ivy and Mara help choose the colors. Together, decide which shapes and colors go into the window and who it’s secretly dedicated to.

  4. 4. Bram’s Secret Toy Workshop

    In the woodworking studio, Bram is carving a surprise toy. Ivy, Mara, and Theo are allowed to sand, paint, or test secret drawers. What kind of toy is it: an animal, a wagon, a puzzle, or something brand new?

  5. 5. Nana Mira’s Patchwork Cloak

    In the fabric and crochet studio, Nana Mira is sewing a magical cloak using tiny scraps from all over the village. Each patch can hold a memory or power: courage, kindness, giggles, or bravery on road trips.

  6. 6. Pop Jasper’s Flower Messages

    Pop Jasper the florist teaches Ivy and Mara how each flower can send a message. Together you can choose which flowers to send to the wizard, the alchemist, or the grandparents and what those flowers mean.

  7. 7. Granny Elen’s Day at the Deli & Sweet Shop

    Granny Elen needs helpers! Ivy, Mara, Theo, and Pip take orders in the cozy deli: sandwiches, soup, and special pastries. Decide what the “Rainkeep Special” is today and who gets the last cookie.

  8. 8. Potions with Grandpa Corwin

    In the alchemist’s shop, Grandpa Corwin mixes safe, silly potions: giggle drops, courage sprinkles, and sleep-tight tea. You and your child can choose three potions and decide what gentle magic each one does.

  9. 9. Library Card Day

    Ivy and Mara visit the village library for their very first library cards. Together you can decide which books they check out: a dragon story, a music book, a map book, or a recipe book for Granny Elen.

  10. 10. A Concert in the Music Room

    In the music room, Ivy plays one instrument, Mara plays another, and even Pip joins in with jingling tags. Choose which instruments they try and what kind of concert they give for the grandparents.

  11. 11. Wren the Wizard’s Lost Spell

    Wren misplaces an important spell page. Ivy, Mara, Theo, and the fairies Lark and Pippin search the library, music room, and village shops to find it. Decide where they look first and who finally spots the missing spell.

  12. 12. Starbloom’s Shimmering Trail

    Starbloom the unicorn trots through Rainkeep at night, leaving a faint sparkling trail. Ivy and Mara follow it from the bedroom, out into the village, and to a special surprise meeting place.

  13. 13. Market Day Mix-Up

    It’s market day on the village street. Flowers, bread, fabrics, and potions all get mixed up! Your child can decide who accidentally takes the wrong basket and how Ivy and Mara help sort everything out.

  14. 14. Tea Party for All the Grandparents

    Ivy and Mara plan a tea party in the living room or garden. Decide which treats Granny Elen bakes, which flowers Pop Jasper brings, what Nana Mira sews for the table, and which potion Grandpa Corwin shares.

  15. 15. Sleepover in the Glass Studio

    During a special night, Ivy and Mara spread blankets in Pebble & Prism Glass Studio. Colored light from the windows paints the walls. What colors do they see, and what gentle stories Selene tells at bedtime?

  16. 16. Road Trip: Forest Campfire

    The whole family takes a road trip to the forest. Move the dolls to your forest scene and decide who gathers sticks, who starts the campfire, and which songs they sing under the stars.

  17. 17. Road Trip: Mountain Overlook

    Ivy, Mara, and Theo drive up into the mountains with Bram. At the overlook, they see the whole village far below. Together you can decide what they notice from so high up and what small treasure they bring back.

  18. 18. Road Trip: Beach Day

    At the beach, Ivy and Mara collect shells, build a sandcastle for Pip, and draw shapes in the sand for Starbloom to visit. Is the water calm or wavy? Do they stay til sunset or come home for snacks?

  19. 19. Road Trip: Desert Caravan

    The friends travel through a soft, golden desert. Maybe they meet a tiny caravan, or they stop at a glowing oasis. You can decide what they pack for the journey and what secret Ivy keeps tucked in her pocket.

  20. 20. The Night the Lights Went Out

    One evening, the lanterns in the village all flicker out. Ivy, Mara, Wren, and the fairies carry candles from room to room, checking on the shops. Together, you can decide how they bring the light back.

  21. 21. Pip’s Missing Collar Mystery

    Pip’s collar goes missing! Ivy and Mara retrace his steps: the music room, the deli, the library, and the glass studio. Your child can choose where they look and which character finally finds the collar.

  22. 22. A Day Off for the Grown-Ups

    All four grandparents and the artists take a day off. Ivy, Mara, and Theo decide how to “run” the village: who bakes, who sews, who waters the plants, and who stirs the pretend potions.

  23. 23. The Library Sleep-In

    Ivy and Mara are allowed to sleep in the library one night. They make a bed of cushions and blankets. Which books do they read, and do the fairies Lark and Pippin sneak in to listen to the stories?

  24. 24. Music Room Talent Show

    Everyone in Rainkeep prepares something small to share: a song, a joke, a magic trick, a dance, or a new recipe. You and your child can decide what each character’s “talent” is and who claps the loudest.

  25. 25. Goodnight, Rainkeep

    At the end of a long day, Ivy and Mara visit each room to say goodnight: to the glass studio, the shops, the library, the music room, and finally the bedroom. Together you can choose one thing each character is grateful for before the lights go out.

Use these prompts as a starting point and let your child lead. There is no wrong way to tell a Rainkeep story.
