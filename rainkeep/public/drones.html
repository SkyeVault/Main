FAQ

What is thermal drone scanning?

Infrared imaging that visualizes temperature differences to reveal heat loss, moisture, or electrical faults. Useful day or night.

What deliverables do I get?

PDF summary with annotated images, optional CSV temperature points, and original media (IR/RGB).

Do you fly legally?

Yes—FAA Part 107 certified. Airspace checks, waivers (if required), and site safety are included in planning.
