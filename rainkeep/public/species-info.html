Black-capped Chickadee Poecile atricapillus 4.7–5.9 in; black cap & bib, white cheeks. Woodland edges, backyards; cavity nester. 6–8 eggs. View
Eastern Bluebird Sialia sialis Medium-sized; blue body, rusty breast. Open fields, meadows. Uses boxes. Often 2 broods. View
Pileated Woodpecker Dryocopus pileatus Large; red crest, black body with white. Mature woods; excavates large cavities. View
American Robin Turdus migratorius 9–11 in; orange breast, gray back. Lawns, forests; open cup nests, 2–3 broods. View
Carolina Wren Thryothorus ludovicianus Small; warm brown, white eyebrow stripe. Dense shrubs, backyards; cavity nester. View
Tree Swallow Tachycineta bicolor Sleek, blue-green; white underside. Open areas, near water; nests in boxes. View
House Finch Haemorhous mexicanus Small, streaked; males red/orange. Urban, feeders; open cup nests. View
