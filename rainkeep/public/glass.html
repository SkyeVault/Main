About the Artist

Combining cutting-edge digital design with traditional glass artistry, Lorelei Noble creates stunning custom templates, patterns, and glass artworks from her studio in Newtown, Pennsylvania. Her expertise in digital fabrication and precision design sets her apart, bringing technical innovation to the ancient craft of glassworking.


Specializing in kiln forming, fusing, and slumping techniques, along with cold working and fabrication, she creates everything from architectural glass components to intricate custom panels. Each piece reflects a perfect marriage of digital precision and handcrafted artistry, transforming spaces with light, color, and expertly designed glass elements.
