About the Artist

With precision and technical skill honed over years of dedicated practice, Lorelei Noble brings both traditional craftsmanship and innovative digital studio techniques to the ancient art of glass working. Based in Newtown, Pennsylvania, she has collaborated with architects, designers, and builders to create stunning window designs and bespoke glass art elements that transform spaces with light and color.


Her work spans from classical leaded glass techniques to cutting-edge fusing and slumping methods, always maintaining the highest standards of artistry and technical excellence. Each piece reflects a deep understanding of how light interacts with glass to create moments of pure beauty.
