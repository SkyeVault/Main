About the Studio

Lorelei Noble is a glassworker based in Newtown, Pennsylvania, blending digital design with traditional glass artistry. She creates professional digital templates, glass art, fused components, and custom pieces from her studio. Her approach combines digital fabrication with traditional techniques like leaded glass, kiln forming, fusing, and cold working, resulting in custom pieces that transform spaces.


Located in the community of Bucks County, Pennsylvania, Lorelei’s vision reflects a connection to the area’s artistic heritage. Her love of digital design allows her to craft precise templates and patterns, while her hands-on glassworking skills produce everything from large panels to delicate custom jewelry pieces.
